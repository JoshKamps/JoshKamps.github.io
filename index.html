Ransomware

Convergence of:

  • Increasing internet + email adoption
  • Increasing personal computer adoption
  • Increasing computing power
  • Online (semi-anonymous) payment systems
  • Increase in amount and importance of data stored

Threat

  • Increasing threat since 2012: 181 million attacks in first 6 months of 2018.
  • WannaCry: organisations running old software, worldwide spread, cost NHS 92 million.

Symantec: “Most dangerous cyber threat”

Characteristics:

  • Attack idea existed previously, but had limitations
    • encryption strength -> now use public key encryption with remote private key storage
    • payment -> now use cryptocurrencies

Prevention:

  • Update software
  • Do not open unknown attachments
  • WannaCry attack was facilitated by an NSA discovered exploit that was leaked and sold on the black market
    • Bug went unreported and was used internally
